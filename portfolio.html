The Challenge

Bryant's Marketing Department lacked a unified digital strategy to attract prospective students, consistently engage current students, and reconnect alumni for long-term value — events, mentorship, and donations — all within a single cohesive system.

Strategy — GOST Framework

  • Built goals across awareness, engagement, and conversion stages
  • Target: 25% increase in website engagement, 40% social following growth
  • Defined 3 personas: prospective student, current student, alumni
  • Tailored content and messaging for each lifecycle stage

Platform Rationale

Instagram & TikTok for awareness and reach. LinkedIn for alumni credibility and engagement. Each platform justified with benchmark data and industry growth rates.

Content System (Not Just Posts)

  • Student takeovers for authentic reach
  • Alumni spotlight carousels for social proof
  • Career tip infographics for value-driven engagement
  • Short-form video series for algorithm-friendly growth
  • Weekly calendar with interaction loops (polls, Q&A, comments)
