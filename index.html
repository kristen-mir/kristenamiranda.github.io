Mock Campaign — Paid Media

Ryze Mushroom Coffee — Meta & Google Ads

Developed a mock paid media campaign for Ryze Mushroom Coffee targeting wellness-focused, career-driven adults ages 20–36. Created two Meta ad variants to A/B test CTA performance and built a Google Search ad using high-intent keywords. Product photography used in the ads was shot by me in the Digital Marketing Lab at Bryant University's Business, Entrepreneurship and Leadership Center using professional lightbox equipment.

Meta Ads — A/B Test

Variant A — "Shop Now"
Standard transactional CTA designed to drive direct conversions from purchase-ready users.
Variant B — "Ryze & Grind"
Brand-personality CTA using Ryze's voice to build connection with hustle-culture audiences.
A/B Test Rationale
Hypothesis

"Ryze & Grind" will outperform "Shop Now" with the 20–36 target by feeling more authentic and less sales-driven.

Audience

Ages 20–36, all genders. Interests: health & wellness, caffeine, marketing, digital marketing, college grads, entry-level careers.

KPIs Tracked

CTR, CPC, conversion rate, ROAS. Winner determined after 7-day test with 1,000+ impressions per variant.

Google Search Ad

Headline

Mushroom Coffee | Focus | No Jitters

Target Keywords

"mushroom coffee", "focus without caffeine crash", "no jitter coffee", "healthy coffee alternative"

Match Type

Phrase match + exact match for high-intent buyers

Goal

Capture users actively searching for caffeine alternatives and drive to product page
